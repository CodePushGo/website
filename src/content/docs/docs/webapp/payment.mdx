CodePushGo billing is managed from the console. The register page creates the account; plan intent is recorded during console onboarding or from organization settings.

## Upgrade a plan

Open [console.codepushgo.com/dashboard/settings/plans](https://console.codepushgo.com/dashboard/settings/plans), choose the plan for the selected organization, and continue to Stripe checkout.

## Are payments secure?

Yes. Payment details are handled by Stripe. CodePushGo does not store credit card numbers.

## Will CodePushGo automatically change my plan?

No. CodePushGo records usage and plan intent, but it does not silently change your selected plan.

## Which organization does a plan affect?

A plan applies to the organization currently selected in the console. Organizations that invited you keep their own billing settings.

## Need a custom plan?

Contact CodePushGo support from the console or email support@codepushgo.com.
